Clinical Note: Calorie calculations use the MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task) formula: Calories = MET × weight(kg) × duration(hrs). Step duration is estimated at ~100 steps/min for stroll to ~130 steps/min for power walking. Waist reduction is estimated at ~1.4–1.8 cm per kg of fat lost (abdominal fat typically constitutes 30–40% of total fat loss). Individual results vary based on diet, metabolism, body composition, and activity pattern. These projections assume walking as the sole activity change.
