## Prompt 1 – BEFORE (central obesity, pattu saree)

Copy and paste this into Grok:

```
Create a single, dignified, photorealistic image of a South Indian woman in her late 30s or early 40s, standing in good indoor lighting. She is wearing a traditional pattu saree (Kanjeevaram or silk), draped elegantly. She has a visible central obesity (belly/waist) that looks natural and not exaggerated. Face is kind and realistic, skin tone typical of South India. Full body or three-quarter view, neutral background. Style: professional, respectful, like a real before-photo for a health program. No text or watermarks. High quality, suitable for a medical wellness website.
```

Save the result as: **`before-after-south-indian-before-1.jpg`**

## Prompt 2 – AFTER (Western covered dress, younger, no wrinkles)

Copy and paste this into Grok:

```
Create a single, dignified, photorealistic image of the same South Indian woman (same person, recognizable), now looking healthier and younger. She is wearing Western but modest, covered clothing (e.g. high-neck blouse with full sleeves, long skirt or palazzo pants, or a modest knee-length dress with sleeves). No visible central obesity; slimmer waist, confident posture. Face: younger appearance, smooth skin, no wrinkles, natural glow. Same indoor lighting and neutral background as the before image. Style: professional, respectful, like a real after-photo for a metabolic wellness program. No text or watermarks. High quality, suitable for a medical wellness website.
```
